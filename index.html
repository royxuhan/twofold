Hi there, Welcome to TwoFold!

TwoFold is a project started at Carnegie Mellon University by four students - Jack, Roxanne, Roy, and Lanna.

We came together because we had a shared vision for a dating app that is built for people who value long-term relationships.

In a time of hooking up and casual dating, we want to challenge the societal expectation of online dating, that is often seen as insincere and phsical.

All in all, we want to make online dating meaningful and impactful, and TwoFold does just that.

Where we started...

To get started with validating our idea, we began with a series of interviews with college students and young adults, who are TwoFold's target customers. We also constructed a persona based on our target demographics.

Our user research involved surveying on general sentiments about online dating, as well as specific questions for those who have existing dating apps on the features they'd like to see.

In addition, we performed a SET analysis to identify if there is a product opportunity gap in the online dating market. We also performed a PESTLE analysis and competitor analysis to further valiadte our idea.

What we learned

Through our research and user interviews, we found a series of opportunites in the online dating market.

Insight #1, most young adults who were interviewed ranked personality and values over looks.

Insight #2, People had varied experience with different dating apps but most did not like Tinder - an app that prioritizes looks.

Insight #3, People prefer a comforting setting when talking to strangers.

Insight #4, The quality of conversations goes down with more number of matches.

Insight #5, An engaging conversation is one that each party responds promptly to a topic of mutual interest.

Pivoting

After constructing an MVP of our vision and pitching it to our fellow classmates, we realized that our initial goal of building long-term relationships is a viable idea, but it can be tricky to implement.

Our initial idea was to have dating coashes and smart algorithms to help us facilitate conversations. This approach inevitably makes TwoFold ingenuine and goes aganist our mission.

MVP

After another interation, we finalized our MVP, and here is the breakdown:

PROFILE CARDS: Users will enter information into 25 profile cards. The high-level metadata will reflect users‚Äô value, interests and personality.

MATCHING: Focus on finding people in the vicinity of their social and physical networks based on two criteria: shared interests and likelihood of organically meeting one another.

GAMIFICATION SYSTEM: When users are first matched, all cards from both sides are hidden. As conversation goes on or touches upon any keyword, relevant profile cards are revealed.

UNLOCK VIDEO CALL: When all cards are revealed for both parties, a video call feature is unlocked for both users. Users can also swap their avatars for profile pictures. This feature rewards users for authentic engagement.

SUGGESTING FIRST DATE: After having their first video call, the app will suggest users to go on a first date. The algorithm will recommend a local event that align with users‚Äô mutual interests.

BUSINESS MODEL: 1-6 month subscription. Everyone has an equal chance and monetization is not tied to giving people unfair advantage such as higher match ranking or visibility.
